# Axiom XII - COGNITIO

## Core Principle

Axiom XII establishes the comprehensive framework for **cognitive sovereignty and enhancement limits**. It ensures that cognitive enhancements are voluntary, informed, reversible, equitable, and protective of human identity, dignity, and cognitive autonomy.

---

## Overview

COGNITIO (Cognition) is the twelfth axiom of the LAIRM framework, establishing comprehensive governance for cognitive enhancement and brain-computer interfaces. This axiom ensures that:

- Cognitive sovereignty is protected and maintained
- Cognitive enhancements are voluntary and informed
- All enhancements are reversible
- Enhancement access is equitable
- Personal identity is preserved
- Cognitive privacy is protected
- All enhancements are audited and monitored
- Enhancement harms are remediated

## Key Concepts

**Cognitive Sovereignty**: Individual autonomy over one's own mind and cognitive processes.

**Informed Consent**: Voluntary agreement based on full disclosure of risks, benefits, and alternatives.

**Reversibility**: Ability to undo enhancements and return to baseline cognitive state.

**Enhancement Equity**: Fair and affordable access to cognitive enhancements.

**Identity Preservation**: Maintenance of personality, values, and self-identity.

---

## Implementation Requirements

### Cognitive Sovereignty
- Voluntary enhancement: No coercion permitted
- Informed consent: Full disclosure required
- Baseline documentation: Pre-enhancement state recorded
- Autonomy protection: Individual choice respected
- Unaugmented protection: Non-enhanced individuals protected

### Enhancement Management
- Reversibility: 30-day reversal capability
- Equity: Affordable access (< 5% annual income)
- Identity preservation: >= 95% personality continuity
- Privacy protection: AES-256 encryption
- Monitoring: Continuous adverse effect detection

### Governance Requirements
- Independent audits: Quarterly verification
- Certification: Annual renewal required
- Reporting: Incident documentation within 24 hours
- Appeals: Independent dispute resolution
- Sanctions: Enforcement of violations

## Compliance Verification

Compliance with Axiom XII is verified through:

1. **Sovereignty Audits**: Verification of cognitive autonomy protection
2. **Consent Audits**: Assessment of informed consent procedures
3. **Reversibility Audits**: Testing of enhancement reversal
4. **Equity Audits**: Verification of fair access
5. **Identity Audits**: Assessment of identity preservation

---

## Sanctions Framework

Non-compliance with cognitive requirements triggers:

- **Enhancement Suspension**: Temporary halt of enhancement operations
- **Forced Reversal**: Mandatory reversal of non-compliant enhancements
- **Enhanced Monitoring**: Increased audit frequency
- **Operational Restrictions**: Limitations on enhancement scope
- **License Revocation**: Permanent removal from operation
