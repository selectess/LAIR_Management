# Impact Analyses

Analyses of the societal, economic, and technological impacts that justify the LAIRM framework.

---

## Content

This directory contains impact analyses and justifications for the LAIRM framework:

- **economic-impact-analysis.md**: Economic impact of autonomous agent failures
- **societal-risk-analysis.md**: Societal risks without regulatory framework
- **technological-necessity-analysis.md**: Technical justification for LAIRM requirements

These analyses demonstrate why the LAIRM framework is necessary and provide evidence-based justification for each axiom.

## Purpose

Impact analyses serve to:
- Justify the necessity of LAIRM framework
- Quantify risks of unregulated autonomous agents
- Demonstrate economic and societal benefits of compliance
- Provide academic foundation for regulatory requirements
