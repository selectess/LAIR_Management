\chapter{Axiom Ψ-VI: AUDITUM}
\label{ch:axiom-VI}

\section*{Foundational Principle}

\begin{principlebox}
All autonomous agent actions must be recorded in tamper-proof, timestamped ledgers. Audit trails are the foundation of accountability and must be immutable, comprehensive, and accessible to regulators.
\end{principlebox}

\section{Overview}

Axiom Ψ-VI establishes mandatory audit trail requirements. Complete, immutable records of all autonomous agent actions enable accountability and enable investigation of failures.

\subsection{Core Obligations}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item All agent actions MUST be logged immutably
    \item Logs MUST include complete context and decision rationale
    \item Logs MUST be retained for minimum 10 years
    \item Logs MUST be accessible to regulatory authorities
    \item Logs MUST be tamper-proof and cryptographically signed
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\section{Article VI.1: Immutable Logging}

\begin{articlebox}[Article VI.1.1: Mandatory Audit Trails]
Every autonomous agent action MUST be logged with:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Agent identifier
    \item Timestamp (UTC, nanosecond precision)
    \item Action type and parameters
    \item Decision rationale and confidence scores
    \item Outcome and consequences
    \item Operator identity (if applicable)
    \item Cryptographic hash of previous entry
\end{enumerate}
\end{articlebox}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\section{Article VI.2: Tamper-Proof Storage}

\begin{articlebox}[Article VI.2.1: Ledger Requirements]
Audit trails MUST be stored in:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Tamper-proof ledgers (blockchain or equivalent)
    \item Distributed across minimum 3 independent jurisdictions
    \item Cryptographically signed by agent and auditor
    \item Accessible to regulatory authorities 24/7
    \item Queryable by agent, timestamp, or action type
\end{enumerate}
\end{articlebox}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\section{Article VI.3: Retention and Access}

\begin{articlebox}[Article VI.3.1: Data Preservation]
Audit trails MUST be:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Retained for minimum 10 years
    \item Accessible to regulatory authorities without delay
    \item Accessible to victims of agent failures
    \item Accessible to independent auditors
    \item Subject to privacy protections for personal data
\end{enumerate}
\end{articlebox}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\section{Article VI.4: Verification and Integrity}

\begin{articlebox}[Article VI.4.1: Audit Trail Verification]
Audit trails MUST be:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Cryptographically signed by agent
    \item Independently verified by auditor
    \item Checked for completeness and consistency
    \item Tested for tamper-evidence
    \item Certified as authentic and unmodified
\end{enumerate}
\end{articlebox}
