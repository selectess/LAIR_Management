\chapter{Axiom Ψ-XIX: IUSTITIA MUNDANA}
\label{ch:axiom-XIX}

\section*{Foundational Principle}

\begin{principlebox}
Autonomous agent governance must promote global justice and equity. The benefits of autonomous systems must be shared equitably across all nations and communities. Developing nations must not be left behind in the autonomous revolution.
\end{principlebox}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\section{Overview}

Axiom Ψ-XIX addresses global equity and justice. Autonomous systems have the potential to exacerbate global inequalities or to promote shared prosperity. This axiom establishes frameworks for ensuring equitable benefits.

\subsection{Core Obligations}

\begin{enumerate}
    \item Benefits of autonomous systems MUST be shared equitably
    \item Developing nations MUST have access to technology
    \item Capacity building MUST be supported
    \item Technology transfer MUST be facilitated
    \item Global governance MUST be inclusive
\end{enumerate}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\section{Article XIX.1: Equitable Benefits}

\begin{articlebox}[Article XIX.1.1: Benefit Sharing]
Autonomous agent governance MUST:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Ensure equitable distribution of benefits
    \item Support development in all nations
    \item Promote technology access
    \item Enable capacity building
    \item Support local innovation
\end{enumerate}
\end{articlebox}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\section{Article XIX.2: Technology Transfer}

\begin{articlebox}[Article XIX.2.1: Knowledge Sharing]
Developed nations MUST:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Share autonomous agent technology
    \item Support capacity building
    \item Facilitate knowledge transfer
    \item Enable local development
    \item Respect intellectual property while enabling access
\end{enumerate}
\end{articlebox}

\vspace{0.5cm}

\section{Article XIX.3: Inclusive Governance}

\begin{articlebox}[Article XIX.3.1: Global Participation]
International governance bodies MUST:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Include all nations in decision-making
    \item Respect sovereignty and self-determination
    \item Support capacity building
    \item Enable meaningful participation
    \item Ensure equitable representation
\end{enumerate}
\end{articlebox}

\vspace{0.5cm}
